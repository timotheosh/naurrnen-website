Patterns of the Enneagram

Type 1: The Reformer

Struggle: Anger and resentment towards imperfection.

Lesson: Embrace imperfection, both in the world and within oneself.

Liberation: Serenity — inner peace and acceptance of life as it is.

Journey: Acceptance

Type 2: The Helper

Struggle: Pride and self-inflation through giving.

Lesson: Recognize that true love includes receiving and honoring one’s own needs.

Liberation: Humility — authentic, unconditional love for self and others.

Journey: Love

Type 3: The Achiever

Struggle: Deceit and striving for external validation.

Lesson: Release the need to perform and embrace authenticity.

Liberation: Truthfulness — living from one’s true identity, not image.

Journey: Release

Type 4: The Individualist

Struggle: Envy and longing for what feels missing.

Lesson: Look inward to find the beauty and completeness already present.

Liberation: Equanimity — emotional balance and self-acceptance.

Journey: Internal Journey

Type 5: The Investigator

Struggle: Avarice and withholding to avoid depletion.

Lesson: Open up to the richness of connection and participation in the world.

Liberation: Generosity — trusting in life’s abundance and sharing freely.

Journey: Abundance

Type 6: The Loyalist

Struggle: Fear and doubt in the face of uncertainty.

Lesson: Trust oneself and life, finding strength even amidst the unknown.

Liberation: Courage — inner faith and decisive action.

Journey: Courage

Type 7: The Enthusiast

Struggle: Gluttony and distraction through endless experiences.

Lesson: Joy arises when we stop running and truly inhabit the present moment.

Liberation: Sobriety — savoring the fullness of now.

Journey: Presence

Type 8: The Challenger

Struggle: Lust and the drive to control or dominate.

Lesson: Strength comes from trusting others and allowing life to unfold naturally.

Liberation: Innocence — openness to vulnerability and authentic strength.

Journey: Openness

Type 9: The Peacemaker

Struggle: Sloth and disengagement from one’s priorities.

Lesson: Step fully into life and bring your unique gifts into the world.

Liberation: Action — awakened energy and meaningful engagement.

Journey: Awaken
